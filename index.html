Notes

How to read

  • pLDDT (0–1 scale): ESMFold per-residue confidence. > 0.7 = well-folded, < 0.5 = disordered/low confidence.
  • TM-score (0–1): structural similarity to the reference. > 0.5 same fold · 0.3–0.5 partial · < 0.3 unrelated.
  • Reference ceiling: 1VPF crystal aligned to ESMFold prediction gives TM = 0.226. That's the practical ceiling for "is this VEGFA?" — anything below means it isn't.
  • The viewers embed every PDB inline so they work offline once the page is loaded. Bandwidth ~1–3 MB per page.

Bottom line

  • No model recreated VEGFA. All generated structures have TM-score ≲ 0.31 vs the crystal — i.e. structurally unrelated.
  • ProtGPT2 t=0.85 reached the highest mean pLDDT (0.48); foundation models are good at plausible proteins, not specific ones.
  • Carbon-3B/8B is ~6× faster than Evo2-7B at this scale but is strand-orientation sensitive — naive reverse-complement sampling collapses to ~98% GC; needs FNS branch or metadata + repetition penalty to stay clean.
  • The real comparison anchor is the experimental RCSB 1VPF crystal of the receptor-binding domain (1.93 Å, 1997).
